Dead Poets Society (1989)

Genre: Drama

Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5)

Robin Williams shines as an unconventional English teacher who inspires his students to think differently, embrace poetry, and “seize the day.” A powerful and emotional classic.

Bridgerton (2020 )

Genre: Romance, Drama, Period

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)

Netflix’s *Bridgerton* brings Regency-era London to life with scandal, romance, and drama. The lavish costumes, diverse cast, and steamy storylines make it an addictive watch.

Get Out (2017)

Genre: Horror, Thriller

Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5)

Jordan Peele’s *Get Out* is a chilling social thriller that blends horror with sharp commentary on race and society. Cleverly written and deeply unsettling, it keeps you on edge until the final scene.

Ginny & Georgia (2021 )

Genre: Drama, Comedy

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5)

*Ginny & Georgia* explores the complex relationship between a mother and daughter navigating love, secrets, and teenage struggles in a small town. It is witty, emotional, and filled with surprising twists that keep viewers hooked.
